UiPath AgentHack 2025 champions are crowned!
 UiPath AgentHack champions_1200x627 1200×628 89.7 KB 
 Out of 400+ bold, creative, and wildly inventive submissions from more than 50 countries all over the world, the very best have risen to the top, and we’re beyond excited to share about who’s taking some awards home! 
 After three adrenaline-packed months of ideation, building, and live demos, UiPath AgentHack 2025 has its winners and below you’ll find the full honours list.
 Why this matters:
 Real-world impact: Every winning agent solves a production-grade problem, grab the use-cases and spin them up today.
 Career rocket fuel: From $10K cash to UiPath certification vouchers, winners walk away with both funding and credibility.
 Community momentum: Even if you didn’t win a prize, you now have a global network of agentic-automation builders ready to collaborate.

• 3rd Place: Accenture Agentic Squad 
 Agentic Testing category prize winners:

 Best Cross-Platform Integration: LUMA - Turkish Airlines Technology

 Most useful feedback: DeepPrompt
 Business ready agent: SoloPath

 Prize breakdown:
 Agent of the future: Grand Prize , $10,000 , Top overall build
 Category 1st , $6,000 , Best solution in each main track
 Category 2nd , $4,000 , Runner-up per track
 Category 3rd , $2,500 , Third place per track
 Special awards , $2,000 each, Business-Ready, Innovative Workflow, Impactful, Advanced
 Cross-category Bonuses , $1,500 each, Community Favorite, Rising Star, Best Agent Builder use, Best Cross-Platform Integration, Best Product Feedback
 That’s over $50,000 in total rewards , not counting the UiPath certification vouchers each winning team takes home.
